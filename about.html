About CleverStack

We got tired of watching good businesses run on bad websites.

CleverStack started as a one-person freelance practice solving the same problem over and over: businesses paying for traffic that landed on a slow, confusing, or outdated site. Today we're a small, senior team that owns the entire build — frontend to database — for founders who'd rather work with one accountable partner than three disconnected vendors.

30+ Projects shipped across web, app & commerce
Mission

Make world-class engineering accessible to businesses that aren't Fortune 500s.

Enterprise-grade performance and design shouldn't be reserved for companies with in-house engineering teams.

Vision

Every client's website should be their hardest-working employee.

A site built right runs a shift for you 24 hours a day, generating leads while you sleep.

What Makes Us Different

Four things most agencies don't offer you.

01

One accountable team, not a relay race

The same people who design your site build it, ship it, and optimize it after launch — nothing gets lost in handoffs between contractors.

02

Business outcomes over deliverables

We measure success in leads, load time, and conversion rate — not just "pages completed." Every decision traces back to revenue.

03

Platform-flexible, not platform-locked

WordPress, Shopify, or fully custom React — we recommend what actually fits your budget and growth stage, not whatever we're used to selling.

04

SEO and performance built in, not bolted on

Speed and search visibility are engineered into the build from day one, not handed off as a separate "marketing" afterthought.
